üìú History & Legacy

Launched in 1996, the Work VS-XX (Double Cross) is perhaps the most iconic and recognizable JDM wheel design ever created. The distinctive 11-spoke step-lip mesh pattern became synonymous with Japanese tuning culture and performance builds worldwide.

The VS-XX was revolutionary for its time, offering deep-dish three-piece construction with exceptional strength-to-weight ratios. It became the wheel of choice for professional drift teams, time attack champions, and street enthusiasts alike. Featured in countless magazines, video games, and movies, the VS-XX defined an era.

Unlike many wheels from its time, the VS-XX remains in production today, a testament to its timeless design. Work continues to offer the VS-XX with modern finishes and expanded sizing, while maintaining the classic aesthetic that made it legendary. It's earned its place as one of the most respected and desired wheel designs in automotive history.

üí° Buyer's Guide

Still in Production: Authentic VS-XX wheels can be ordered new directly from Work or authorized dealers. Lead times vary from 3-6 months depending on specifications. New sets start around $4,000-$7,000 USD depending on size and finish.

Used Market: Due to popularity, VS-XX replicas are extremely common. Verify authenticity by checking for Work stamps on barrels, serial numbers, and quality of construction. Genuine used sets typically sell for $2,000-$5,000 depending on condition and specs.
